Instructions to Get Company Logos

For Proof of Skill (proofofskill.org):

  1. Go to https://proofofskill.org
  2. Right-click on the page and select "View Page Source" or press Ctrl+U
  3. Look for logo images in the HTML (search for "logo", "brand", or "icon")
  4. Save the logo image as "proofofskill-logo.png" in the company folder
  5. Alternative: Take a screenshot of their logo and crop it to 64x64px or 128x128px

For Naga Soft Labs (nagasoftlabs.com):

  1. Go to https://nagasoftlabs.com
  2. Right-click on their logo and select "Save Image As"
  3. Save as "nagasoft-logo.png" in the company folder
  4. I noticed they use "NSL" branding, so look for that logo
